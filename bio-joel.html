Joel
Blanchet

Buffalo | New York City | Chicago

Joel Blanchet is a trial lawyer who represents companies, executives, and institutions in litigation, investigations, and other disputes across the country. His practice is broad by design, with experience spanning commercial litigation, white collar and regulatory matters, environmental disputes, product liability, class actions, and technically demanding cases.

Before helping build Blanchet, Joel spent more than a decade as a partner in the litigation department at Kirkland & Ellis and later helped lead a litigation practice at Phillips Lytle. Over the course of his career, he has handled disputes involving toxic tort liability, complex financial transactions, mergers and acquisitions, securities, intellectual property, bankruptcy, and government-facing investigations.

Joel is known for direct involvement, trial readiness, and a practical approach to difficult disputes. He works closely with clients from the outset of a matter, with an emphasis on clear decision-making, disciplined case management, and preparation that creates leverage at every stage.

REPRESENTATIVE EXPERIENCE

Joel's public experience includes:

  • Representing BP in matters related to the Deepwater Horizon accident and oil spill
  • Representing Dow in merger litigation arising from the Rohm and Haas transaction, which settled on the first day of trial with a reduction in the cash consideration to be paid
  • Handling a range of business, tort, securities, IP, and bankruptcy disputes for major corporate clients across the country
